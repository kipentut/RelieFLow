Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Helping you understand our mission and how you can get involved

GENERAL INFO VOLUNTEERING & INVOLVEMENT

What is RelieFlow?

ReliefFlow functions as a disaster relief organization which distributes urgent resources such as food water pain medications and shelter to areas affected by natural disasters through rapid and effective methods.

How can I volunteer?

Our website enables you to register for volunteering alongside community outreach programs and their existing volunteer applications. The organization provides three service opportunities which involve relief goods packing and logistics activities.

Who runs RelieFlow?

The ReliefFlow operation runs under dedicated humanitarian professionals paired with local community leaders and volunteers who work together with established NGOs and public agencies.

Do I need experience to help?

Absence of qualifications or experience enables anyone to join with their commitment and available time. Our organization delivers concise orientation programs to introduce all new volunteers to our activities.

Where do you Operate?

The organization operates across disaster-prone parts of the Philippines through mobile teams prepared for immediate deployment to areas needing assistance.

Can I start a fundraiser with RelieFlow?

Yes! We support community-led fundraisers. The platform enables you to register your initiative with our team who will provide guidance.
DONATIONS RECEIVING HELP

How can I donate?

The official GCash QR code on our website’s “Donate” page offers you the ability to make a donation. Our donation method through GCash QR code represents the sole accepted method for contributions at this time.

I was affected by a disaster. How can I get help?

Approval for enrollment is available by visiting your local barangay centers or through the app interface. We collaborate with local leaders for identifying individuals in need as well as establishing their priority level.

Where does my donation go?

Every donation literally reaches families who need food supplies and clean drinking water along with sanitation items and protective shelter and essential assistance. Some disaster relief money supports training about disaster preparedness in addition to running environmental recovery programs within vulnerable communities.

What kind of aid does RelieFlow provide?

Our organization delivers emergency food packages along with water as well as medical services and hygiene supplies and temporary shelter facilities and operational aid.

Can I donate goods instead of money?

Absolutely! Non-perishable food together with clothing along with blankets and hygiene products and school supplies are all acceptable donation items.

Do I need to register?

Yes. Accurate and efficient distribution of aid depends on the registration system. Local authorities offer assistance for registration which you can complete either online or through their channels.
